Firefox Adventron 27.5 Electric Bike Product Overview

Frame

The Firefox Adventron 27.5 Electric Bike is an E-Bicycle which has an Alloy Hardtail MTB Frame.

Gearing

The Firefox Adventron 27.5 Electric Bike has a Shimano Altus M310 7-Speed rear derailleur and Shimano TX50 shifter.

Brakes

This Electric Bicycle from Firefox comes with Tektro M300 Mechanical Disc Brakes.

Battery

The Firefox Adventron 27.5 Electric Bike has a 36V, 8.7Ah Removable Li-ion battery with Panasonic Cells.

Motor

The Firefox Adventron 27.5 Electric Bike has a rear hub driven motor which has a mileage of 35kms on one full charge.

Fork/Suspension

The Firefox Adventron 27.5 Electric Bike has a Suntour XCT Suspension Fork with a 100mm travel and Hydraulic Lockout.

Tires

The Firefox Adventron 27.5 Electric Bike comes with a pair of Ralson 27.5" x 2.1" tires which are attached to Alloy Rims.

Price

The price of the Firefox Adventron 27.5 Electric Bike is Rs.60,000.

Who should buy this Bicycle?

The Firefox Adventron 27.5 Electric Bike is a performance E-MTB which is best suited for trail riding as well as commuting in the city.
